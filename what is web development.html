Web development is the process of creating websites and web applications. It involves a range of activities such as designing, coding, deploying, and maintaining websites. The development process involves working with various technologies, such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and various server-side languages. Web developers are responsible for building and maintaining websites, and web applications, according to their clients' requirements. They must also ensure that websites are user-friendly and perform optimally. Web developers often collaborate with web designers to create attractive, user-friendly websites.
