Online surveys are a structured questionnaire sent out over the internet to a target sample. They are used to collect data in order to gain insights into customers' and employees' needs and preferences.
Benefits of online surveys include the ability to collect information from a broad audience for very little cost, as well as the opportunity to make improvements to a website or business based on the data collected.
Best practices for developing online surveys include designing questions before collecting data, providing respondents with estimates of completion time, and including a mix of open-ended and closed questions. [1][2]
