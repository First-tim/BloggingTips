If you're looking to start selling digital products, you should first think about what products you want to offer. Do you have any existing content that you can transform into digital products that you could sell? This could include eBooks, audio/video recordings, software, or any other type of digital content. Once you have an idea of what you want to sell, you can then move on to setting up a website or an online store to sell your digital products. This could include setting up a blog, an online shop, or a marketplace. Your online platform should be designed to be user-friendly and attractive in order to maximize sales.

Additionally, you will need to set up an online payment system in order to receive payments from customers. Popular online payment systems include PayPal, Stripe, and Apple Pay. Once your online store is set up and ready to go, you can begin marketing your products. You can use social media, email marketing, and other digital tactics to reach out to potential customers. Additionally, creating content such as blog posts and videos can help you gain visibility and draw in customers. With the right strategies, you can create and successfully sell digital products. Good luck!
