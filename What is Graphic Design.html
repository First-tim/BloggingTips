Graphic design is the process of visual communication and problem-solving using one or more of typography, photography, illustration, and other visual elements. Graphic design is used in a variety of projects, from creating logos and advertisements to developing websites and promotional materials.
Graphic designers use typography, color, shapes, and images to create a visual representation of messages and ideas. They may also use various tools, such as computer software, to create the final product.
Graphic design is an important part of any business’s marketing and advertising strategy, as it helps to create a memorable and engaging brand identity. Graphic designers must have a solid understanding of color, composition, and typography to effectively communicate a company’s message to its target audience.
