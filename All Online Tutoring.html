the second answer is how to start online tutoring

Starting an online tutoring business can be a great way to make money and help others at the same time. To get started, you’ll need to have a good understanding of the subject matter and the ability to communicate well with students. You’ll also need a computer and reliable internet connection.

You’ll need to create a profile on a tutoring platform, such as Tutor.com or TutorU. Here, you’ll be able to upload your resume, list your qualifications and experience, and create a rate that you are willing to charge for your services. You’ll also need to provide a brief bio and a photo of yourself.

Once your profile is set up, you’ll need to find students in need of tutoring. You can post on social media, reach out to friends and family, or even attend local events or fairs to find potential students.

Once you’ve found students, you’ll need to schedule sessions and discuss payment arrangements. You’ll also need to make sure that you have the proper materials and resources to provide effective tutoring.
