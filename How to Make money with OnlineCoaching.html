The primary way to make money with online coaching is to charge a fee for your services. This can be done in a variety of ways, such as through a pay-as-you-go model or a subscription-based model. Additionally, you can offer additional services such as ebooks, webinars, and online courses. You can also expand your client base by offering group coaching and workshops. Finally, you can generate additional revenue through affiliate marketing, where you partner with companies to promote their products and services on your website in exchange for a commission.
